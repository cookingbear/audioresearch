Audio samples from "IMPROVING GAN-BASED VOCODER FOR FAST AND HIGH-QUALITY SPEECH SYNTHESIS"

Paper: arXiv
Authors: Mengnan He, Tingwei Guo, Yuqing Zhang, Zhu Li, Ruixiong Zhang, Caixia Gong
Abstract: Following tremendous success in the Generative Adversarial Network(GAN), the vocoders based on GAN have recently shown much faster speed of speech generation. However, compared to the auto-regressive models, the quality of gen- erated speech is slightly inferior, and the real-time factor (RTF) still can’t be satisfied in some scenes, such as human- computer dialogue, map navigation running on devices with limited resources. To address the issues, we propose a new GAN based vocoder model. Firstly, we introduce the Shuffle- Residual Block into the generator to get a lower RTF and then use time-domain loss to get a higher speech quality. Secondly, we propose a Frequency Transformation Block in the discriminator to capture the correlation between differ- ent frequency bins in every frame. In our experiments, our model shows a lower RTF and higher speech quality than MB-MelGAN and HiFi-GAN V2. To the best of our knowl- edge, our model achieves the state-of-the-art RTF of the GAN models under the premise of ensuring the speech quality.
